What`s MyFiles?

MyFiles is a reliable, safe, affordable and easy-to-use Telegram bot for sharing all kinds of files (photos, videos, audios, documents). Digital information is expanding daily, MyFiles allows you to upload, save and share files of any size, anywhere, anytime.

Practical usage

About the bot

Work Files: For freelancers and small businesses, MyFiles makes it easy and secure to share documents with clients and partners.

Studying at home: For distant learners, MyFiles will help you save and share homework and study materials.

Travel arrangements: Keeping tickets, hotel reservations and other travel documents is easier and more convenient with MyFiles. Photo Albums: You can use MyFiles to comfortably store and share or holiday photos
